About Dullroar Enterprises

Jim Lehmer started consulting under the name Dullroar Enterprises since 1983. Jim Lehmer has spent over three decades in various software development roles on a variety of operating systems and programming languages. He specializes in making systems that were never meant to talk to each other talk to each other.

Employees

Jim Lehmer

Jim is the sole proprietor of Dullroar Enterprises.

He currently lives in the mountains of northern New Mexico. When he isn't consulting or writing, he is usually out playing somewhere in the Rocky Mountains - hiking, climbing, backpacking, snowshoeing, and other things much more fun than dealing with data! He is also a twice-published O'Reilly author.
